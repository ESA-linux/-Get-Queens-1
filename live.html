LibrePlanet 2020 - Live

The conference is live!

Please note that all online participants in LibrePlanet 2020 interactions are expected to abide by the conference's safe space policy.

To join other channels, such as #libreplanet_room_grand, copy and paste this text into the IRC client above after you connect to IRC: /join #libreplanet_room_grand

Support the FSF!

Hard-working FSF hackers are running our advanced, fully free software streaming system for LibrePlanet 2020. We appreciate your support to help cover labor and equipment.

Visit the FSF Shop!

LibrePlanet 2020 is online this year, but that doesn't mean that you have to miss out on the annual conference T-shirt or any of the other GNU and FSF gear! Let the world know you were "here" by picking up this year's T-shirt in the FSF shop.
