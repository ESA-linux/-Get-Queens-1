TRANSCENDENCE is a new docu-series that explores the current state of our physical, emotional, and mental health in a captivating new way.

Drawing on research and interviews by some of the world’s most recognized celebrities, doctors, and thought leaders including Novak Djokovic, Jim Kwik, Mark Hyman, Libby Weaver, Bruce Lipton, Wim Hof, Gabrielle Bernstein, Joe Dispenza, and Marie Forleo to name a few, this inspirational FMTV original series is set to challenge the way you view your health and ultimately question how you can live a better life.

Spanning over five fascinating episodes that individually tackle some of the biggest challenges of our time, TRANSCENDENCE takes the viewer on a journey into the ‘self’ like no other docu-series before it.

TRANSCENDENCE will show you how to:

  • Optimize your diet to heal your gut and autoimmune conditions naturally.
  • Control stress, fear, and anxiety in your life with simple daily habits.
  • Overcome self doubt & limiting beliefs that are holding you back in life.
  • Discover your true passion & connect with your life’s purpose.
  • Uncover what you are truly capable of mentally, physically, and spiritually.
  • Reprogram your mind & body to live a healthier and happier life.

“All great things begin on the inside. You have greatness inside of you. Now is the time to unlock it.”

Jim Kwik – TRANSCENDENCE, Episode 3

Episodes

EPISODE 1

What’s in Our Food?

Could the first step to changing your life be as simple as changing what you put in your mouth? Optimize your diet to fuel your body, heal your gut and autoimmune conditions, and reach your true physical and mental potential.

EPISODE 2

Overcoming Fear & Stress

Discover why living in a state of constant stress and overwhelm could be suppressing your immune system and altering your physiology. Learn how to control stress, fear, and anxieties in your life with simple daily habits.

EPISODE 3

Beyond Belief

What you think, you become. Learn how to reprogram your mind with effective and practical strategies to overcome your limitations and change the impossible to possible.

EPISODE 4

Whose Goals Are We Chasing?

Happiness is not a result of an achievement or a goal met. Happiness is what propels you to achieve your goals. But before you start, ask yourself, whose goals are you really chasing?

EPISODE 5

The Art of Fulfillment

Learn the secret art of fulfillment and how to discover your true purpose and passion in life. Make a promise to yourself to start living a life that’s extraordinary to you and excel at what you love doing.
